While experts caution against panic, they also advise organizations to review
any plans and strategies in place to allow for social distancing.

Ask yourself these questions:

What’s your plan in the event of a full shutdown of your operations? How will your employees continue to work?
What’s the plan if a key business unit is shut down? Are you able to recover it quickly? 
What’s the plan in the event that key members of your team are forced into a quarantine situation? Can they continue to function in their day-to-day roles?
What’s the plan if one of your remote offices is in a highly impacted part of the country?
